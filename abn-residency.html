ABN Residency, Antarvedi

Comfortable and convenient stay just 450 meters from Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple

ABN Residency is a simple, clean, and budget-friendly accommodation located just 450 meters from Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple, Antarvedi. It is an ideal stay option for pilgrims, families, and devotees who wish to stay very close to the temple while enjoying a peaceful and comfortable environment. The residency provides easy access to the temple, sangamam, beach, and nearby local areas, making it a convenient choice for short and extended visits.
