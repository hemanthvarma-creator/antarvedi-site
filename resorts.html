Resorts Around Antarvedi

Comfortable stays by the Godavari River, coconut groves, and serene backwaters

The region around Antarvedi and nearby Dindi village offers some of the best riverside and backwater resort experiences in Andhra Pradesh. Whether you seek luxurious comfort, peaceful nature retreats, or riverfront relaxation, these resorts provide memorable stays.
