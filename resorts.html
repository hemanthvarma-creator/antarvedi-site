Resorts Around Antarvedi

Comfortable stays by the Godavari River, coconut groves, and serene backwaters

The region around Antarvedi and nearby Dindi village offers some of the best riverside and backwater resort experiences in Andhra Pradesh. Whether you seek luxurious comfort, peaceful nature retreats, or riverfront relaxation, these resorts provide memorable stays with views of the mighty Godavari, coconut groves, and calm lagoons.

Top Resorts You Can Stay At

Dindi RVR, On the Godavari

  • Premium resort along the Godavari River with scenic views
  • Swimming pool, restaurant, games room & fitness centre
  • Indoor and outdoor activities for families and kids
  • Air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities and river views
  • Great choice for leisure stays and river retreats

Dindi Haritha Coconut Country Resort

  • Riverside resort set amidst coconut trees and backwaters
  • Restaurant, bar, swimming pool, and spa services
  • Boating and nature walks available nearby
  • Ideal for family vacations and tranquil getaways
  • Rooms often come with river or lagoon views

Sterling – Dindi By The Godavari

  • Part of the Sterling Holiday Resorts network with quality services
  • Classic, Privilege & Premier rooms with river views
  • Multi-cuisine restaurant and sunset bar available
  • Swimming pool and spa for relaxation
  • Perfect setting for couples, families & leisure travellers

Note: Resort facilities, pricing, and availability may vary seasonally. It’s recommended to book in advance and check with the resort for offers, packages, and on-site activities. Enjoy peaceful evenings by the river and explore nearby attractions like beaches, temples, and boat rides.
