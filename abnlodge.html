ABN Lodge, Antarvedi

Comfortable and convenient stay just 450 meters from Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple

ABN Lodge is a simple, clean, and budget-friendly accommodation located just 450 meters from Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple, Antarvedi. It is an ideal stay option for pilgrims, families, and devotees who wish to stay very close to the temple while enjoying a peaceful and comfortable environment. The lodge provides easy access to the temple, sangamam, beach, and nearby local areas, making it a convenient choice for short and extended visits.

Note: Room availability and pricing may vary based on temple events, festivals, and weekends. Visitors are advised to contact the lodge directly before arrival, especially during peak pilgrimage seasons.
