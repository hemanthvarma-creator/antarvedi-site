Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple

Antarvedi Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple is a sacred shrine located at the confluence of the Godavari River and the Bay of Bengal. The temple is believed to be established by Sage Vasishta and is one of the most important pilgrimage centers in coastal Andhra Pradesh.

Darshan Timings

  • Morning Darshan: 6:00 AM to 12:30 PM
  • Evening Darshan: 3:00 PM to 8:00 PM

Timings may vary on festival days and special occasions.

Abhishekams

  • Daily Abhishekam: 6:00 AM to 7:00 AM
  • Special Abhishekams on Ekadasi, Saturdays, and festival days
  • Abhishekam tickets are issued at the temple counter

Poojas and Sevas

  • Suprabhata Seva
  • Archana
  • Alankara Seva
  • Nitya Kalyanam
  • Sahasranama Archana

Annadanam

Free Annadanam is provided to devotees daily at the temple premises. On festival days, Annadanam is organized on a larger scale for all visiting pilgrims.

  • Lunch Timings: 12:00 PM to 3:00 PM

Book Darshan

Darshan and Seva bookings are managed through the official AP Temples website.

Book Darshan Online

Bookings are handled only on the official AP Temples portal.
