Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple

A sacred pilgrimage site where the holy Godavari meets the Bay of Bengal

Book Darshan

The Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple at Antarvedi is an ancient and spiritually significant pilgrimage center in Andhra Pradesh. Believed to be established by Sage Vasishta, the temple attracts devotees from across the country who come seeking blessings, peace, and divine grace.

Temple Timings & Schedules

  • Temple Opens: 6:00 AM
  • Morning Darshan: 6:00 AM – 12:30 PM
  • Afternoon Break: 1:00 PM – 3:30 PM
  • Evening Darshan: 3:30 PM – 7:30 PM

Abhishekams

  • Daily Abhishekam: 8:00 AM – 9:30 AM
  • Special Abhishekams on Ekadasi & festival days
  • Tickets available at temple counters

Poojas & Sevas

  • Suprabhata Seva
  • Archana
  • Sahasranama Archana
  • Nitya Kalyanam
  • Special Sevas during festivals

Annadanam

  • Annadanam Timings: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM
  • Free meals served daily to devotees
  • Extended Annadanam during festivals

Note: Temple timings and sevas may change on festival days or special occasions. Please verify details on the official AP Temples website before planning your visit.
