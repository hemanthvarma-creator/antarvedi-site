How to Reach Antarvedi

A complete guide to reach Antarvedi by road, rail, air, and river routes

Antarvedi is located in the Konaseema region of Andhra Pradesh and is well connected through nearby towns and transport hubs. Though the final stretch is peaceful and rural, proper planning ensures a smooth and comfortable journey for pilgrims and travelers alike.

Travel Options

By Road

  • Well connected via Narsapur, Palakollu, and Amalapuram
  • APSRTC buses operate from major towns
  • Private taxis available from Rajahmundry and Kakinada
  • Last stretch offers scenic village and canal views

By Train

  • Nearest railway stations: Narsapur and Palakollu
  • Rajahmundry is the nearest major junction
  • Buses and taxis available from stations
  • Recommended for long-distance travelers

By Air

  • Nearest airport: Rajahmundry Airport
  • Well connected to Hyderabad, Bengaluru, and Chennai
  • Taxi journey from airport takes approx 3–4 hours
  • Best option for travelers from distant cities

Ferry & Boat Route

  • Popular ferry service from the Narsapur side
  • Crossing the Godavari is a unique experience
  • Frequently used by pilgrims and locals
  • Availability depends on river conditions

Local Transport Tips

  • Autos and local jeeps available in nearby towns
  • Pre-book return transport during peak days
  • Limited night transport facilities
  • Weekends and festivals attract heavy crowds

Note: Antarvedi is a sacred and eco-sensitive destination. Visitors are advised to plan return journeys in advance, follow local guidelines, respect temple traditions, and avoid littering to preserve the natural beauty of the region.
