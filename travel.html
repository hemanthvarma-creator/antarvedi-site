How to Reach
Antarvedi

A complete guide — road, rail, air, and river routes to the sacred confluence

Antarvedi is located in the Konaseema region of Andhra Pradesh. Well connected through nearby towns and transport hubs, the final stretch is peaceful and rural — proper planning ensures a smooth, comfortable journey for pilgrims and travelers alike.

Travel Options

🚗 By Road

  • Via Narsapur, Palakollu & Amalapuram
  • APSRTC buses from major towns
  • Private taxis from Rajahmundry & Kakinada
  • Scenic village and canal views en route

🚂 By Train

  • Nearest stations: Narsapur & Palakollu
  • Rajahmundry is the nearest major junction
  • Buses and taxis from railway stations
  • Recommended for long-distance travelers

✈️ By Air

  • Nearest airport: Rajahmundry Airport
  • Connected to Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Chennai
  • Taxi from airport: approx 3–4 hours
  • Best option for distant city travelers

⛵ Ferry & Boat

  • Ferry service from the Narsapur side
  • Crossing the Godavari — a unique experience
  • Frequently used by pilgrims and locals
  • Availability depends on river conditions

🛺 Local Transport

  • Autos and local jeeps in nearby towns
  • Pre-book return transport on peak days
  • Limited night transport facilities
  • Weekends and festivals attract heavy crowds

Note: Antarvedi is a sacred and eco-sensitive destination. Plan return journeys in advance, follow local guidelines, respect temple traditions, and avoid littering to preserve the natural beauty.
