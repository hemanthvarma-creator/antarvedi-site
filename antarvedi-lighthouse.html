Antarvedi
Lighthouse

A thrilling climb to panoramic views of sea, river, forests, and endless sky

The Antarvedi Lighthouse offers one of the most breathtaking viewpoints in the region. Standing tall near the coastline, it invites visitors to climb its spiral steps and experience a refreshing blend of adventure, greenery, and endless water horizons from the top.

What Makes the Lighthouse Special

🧗 The Climb

  • Approximately 101–111 steps to the top
  • Exciting spiral staircase inside
  • Perfect mini-adventure for all ages
  • Entry ticket around ₹10–₹20

🌅 The View

  • 360° views of the Bay of Bengal
  • Godavari River flowing to Sangamam
  • Fishing ponds and mangrove waterways
  • Green landscapes blending with water

🌬️ The Experience

  • Strong, refreshing sea winds at the top
  • Cool and peaceful atmosphere
  • Feels like the world is just water and sky
  • Ideal for photography & quiet reflection

Note: Entry timings and ticket prices may change. Be cautious while climbing, follow safety instructions, and avoid visiting during extreme weather conditions.
