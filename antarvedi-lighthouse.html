Antarvedi Lighthouse

A thrilling climb to panoramic views of sea, river, forests, and sky

The Antarvedi Lighthouse offers one of the most breathtaking viewpoints in the region. Standing tall near the coastline, it invites visitors to climb its steps and experience a refreshing blend of adventure, greenery, and endless water views from the top.

What Makes the Lighthouse Special

🧗 An Adventurous Climb

  • Approximately 101–111 steps to the top
  • Exciting spiral climb inside the lighthouse
  • Perfect mini-adventure for all ages
  • Entry ticket priced around ₹10–₹20

🌅 Panoramic Views

  • Clear views of the Bay of Bengal beach
  • Godavari River flowing into Sagar Sangamam
  • Surrounding fishing ponds and waterways
  • Green landscapes blending with water bodies

🌬️ Experience at the Top

  • Strong, refreshing sea winds
  • Cool and peaceful atmosphere
  • Feels like greenery and water everywhere
  • Ideal spot for photos and relaxation

Note: Entry timings and ticket prices may change based on local authority regulations. Visitors are advised to be cautious while climbing, follow safety instructions, and avoid visiting during extreme weather conditions.
