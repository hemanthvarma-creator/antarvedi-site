Antarvedi Beach

A serene coastline where river breeze meets the vast Bay of Bengal

Antarvedi Beach is a peaceful and less-crowded coastal stretch known for its clean sands, gentle waves, and calming atmosphere. Unlike commercial beaches, it offers a raw and natural seaside experience where visitors can relax, enjoy scenic views, and feel close to nature.

Things to Do at the Beach

Beach Activities

  • Horse riding along the shoreline
  • Buggy car rides on the sand
  • Beach walks and leisure strolls
  • Photography and sunset viewing

Experience Highlights

  • Wide open beach with minimal crowd
  • Cool sea breeze throughout the day
  • Golden sunsets over the horizon
  • Relaxing ambience ideal for families

Visitor Information

  • Best Time: Early morning & evening
  • Season: October to March
  • Activities available during peak hours
  • Local vendors operate on weekends

Note: Swimming is not advised due to unpredictable sea currents. Visitors are encouraged to follow safety instructions, avoid venturing deep into the water, and support local service providers responsibly.
