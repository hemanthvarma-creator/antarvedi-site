Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple, Antarvedi, is a revered ancient shrine located at the sacred confluence of the Godavari River and the Bay of Bengal. Believed to be established by Sage Vasishta, the temple attracts thousands of devotees every year who seek divine blessings, spiritual peace, and fulfillment through sacred rituals and darshan.

Temple Timings & Rituals

Darshan Timings

  • Temple Opens: 6:00 AM
  • Morning Darshan: 6:00 AM – 12:30 PM
  • Afternoon Break: 1:00 PM – 3:30 PM
  • Evening Darshan: 3:30 PM – 7:30 PM

Abhishekams

  • Daily Abhishekam: 8:00 AM – 9:30 AM
  • Special Abhishekams are performed on Ekadasi and festival days

Annadanam

  • Annadanam Timings: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM
  • Free meals are served daily to all devotees

Note: Temple timings and rituals may vary during festivals and special occasions. Devotees are advised to verify the latest schedule through the official AP Temples website before planning their visit.
