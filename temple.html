Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple

A sacred pilgrimage site at the confluence of the Godavari River and the Bay of Bengal

Book Darshan

Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple, Antarvedi, is an ancient and revered shrine located at the sacred confluence of the Godavari River and the Bay of Bengal. Established by Sage Vasishta, the temple attracts thousands of devotees seeking divine blessings and spiritual peace.

Temple Timings & Rituals

Darshan Timings

  • Temple Opens: 6:00 AM
  • Morning Darshan: 6:00 AM – 12:30 PM
  • Afternoon Break: 1:00 PM – 3:30 PM
  • Evening Darshan: 3:30 PM – 7:30 PM

Abhishekams

  • Daily Abhishekam: 8:00 AM – 9:30 AM
    • Special Abhishekams on Ekadasi and festival days

Annadanam

  • Timings: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM
    • Free meals served daily to all devotees

Note: Timings may change during festivals and special occasions. Please verify details through the official AP Temples website.
