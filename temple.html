Antarvedi Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple

The Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple at Antarvedi is a sacred and ancient pilgrimage site located where the holy River Godavari meets the Bay of Bengal. Devotees from all over visit here to receive blessings, experience the spiritual atmosphere, and participate in temple rituals and sevas.

Temple Timings & Schedules

Daily Open / Close:

  • Temple Opens: 6:00 AM
  • Morning Darshan: 6:00 AM – 12:30 PM
  • Afternoon Break: 1:00 PM – 3:30 PM
  • Evening Darshan: 3:30 PM – 7:30 PM

Abhishekam (Ritual Bath):

  • Abhishekam Pooja (Daily): 8:00 AM – 9:30 AM
  • Special Abhishekam (Festivals): Timings may vary

Annadanam (Free Food):

  • Annadanam Distribution: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM
  • Festive Days Annadanam: May extend

Other Rituals & Poojas:

  • Nijaroopa Darshan (Near Deity Viewing): 6:00 AM – 8:00 AM
  • Sarva Darshan (General Viewing): 9:30 AM – 12:30 PM & 3:30 PM – 7:30 PM
  • Sudarsana Homam: Around 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  • Special Sevas (Kalyanotsavam, Laksharchana): As per temple announcements

Note: Timings can change during festival days or special temple occasions. Please verify the official schedule before planning your visit.
