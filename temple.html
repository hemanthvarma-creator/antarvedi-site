Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple

A sacred pilgrimage site where the holy Godavari meets the Bay of Bengal

Book Darshan

The Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple at Antarvedi is a sacred and ancient pilgrimage site located where the holy River Godavari meets the Bay of Bengal. Devotees from all over visit here to receive blessings, experience the spiritual atmosphere, and participate in temple rituals and sevas.

Temple Timings & Schedules

Daily Open / Close:

  • Temple Opens: 6:00 AM
  • Morning Darshan: 6:00 AM – 12:30 PM
  • Afternoon Break: 1:00 PM – 3:30 PM
  • Evening Darshan: 3:30 PM – 7:30 PM

Abhishekam:

  • Daily Abhishekam: 8:00 AM – 9:30 AM
  • Festival Abhishekams: Timings may vary

Annadanam:

  • Annadanam Timings: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM

Note: Timings may change during festivals. Please verify on the official AP Temples website.
