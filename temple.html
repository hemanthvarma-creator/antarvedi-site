Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple at Antarvedi is an ancient and revered pilgrimage destination, situated at the sacred confluence of the Godavari River and the Bay of Bengal. The temple is believed to be established by Sage Vasishta and is visited by thousands of devotees seeking divine blessings, peace, and spiritual fulfillment.

Temple Timings & Ritual Schedule

Darshan Timings

  • Temple Opening: 6:00 AM
  • Morning Darshan: 6:00 AM – 12:30 PM
  • Afternoon Break: 1:00 PM – 3:30 PM
  • Evening Darshan: 3:30 PM – 7:30 PM

Abhishekams

  • Daily Abhishekam: 8:00 AM – 9:30 AM
  • Special Abhishekams on Ekadasi and festival days

Annadanam

  • Timings: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM
  • Free meals served daily to all devotees

Timings and rituals may vary during festivals or special occasions. Devotees are advised to confirm the latest schedule through the official AP Temples website before planning their visit.
