Apple Resorts, Antarvedi

A peaceful beachside stay near Antarvedi Temple, surrounded by nature and serenity

Apple Resorts in Antarvedi is a calm and comfortable beachside resort located close to the sacred Antarvedi Temple and the scenic Godavari coastline. Designed for pilgrims, families, and leisure travelers, the resort offers clean rooms, a peaceful atmosphere, and easy access to the beach, temple, and nearby attractions. It is an ideal choice for visitors looking to relax, reconnect with nature, and experience Antarvedi at a slow, peaceful pace.
