    Godavari
    Boating

    Explore the river, mangrove forests, and sacred confluence through unforgettable boat journeys

    Know More 📞 Book on WhatsApp

    Boating at Antarvedi offers a unique way to experience the beauty and spirituality of the Godavari. From sacred pilgrimage routes to calm mangrove corridors and thrilling speed rides — boating here caters to devotees, nature lovers, and adventure seekers alike.

    Types of Boating Experiences

    🚤 Tourist Boating

    • Boat service to Sagar Sangamam
    • Route via Anna Chellula Gattu
    • Popular among pilgrims and families
    • Calm ride suitable for all ages

    🌿 Mangrove Forest

    • Boat through ancient mangrove corridors
    • Peaceful, scenic, nature-rich experience
    • Ideal for photography and mindfulness
    • Less crowded — highly recommended

    ⚡ Speed Boating

    • High-speed ride on the Godavari waters
    • Short-duration thrill experience
    • Available in favorable weather
    • Safety jackets provided for all riders
    📞 Book Boating on WhatsApp

    Note: Boating availability depends on river conditions and weather. Timings and prices vary seasonally. Follow safety guidelines, wear life jackets, and book through authorized local operators only.
