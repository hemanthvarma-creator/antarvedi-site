Godavari Boating

Explore the River, Mangrove Forests, and Sacred Confluence through unforgettable boat journeys

Know More Book Now

Boating at Antarvedi offers a unique way to experience the beauty and spirituality of the Godavari River. From sacred pilgrimage routes to calm mangrove forests surroundings and thrilling speed rides, boating here caters to devotees, nature lovers, and adventure seekers alike.

Types of Boating Experiences

Regular Tourist Boating

  • Boat service to Sagar Sangamam
  • Route includes Anna Chellula Gattu
  • Popular among pilgrims and families
  • Calm and steady ride suitable for all ages

Mangrove Forest

  • Boat travels through river center surrounded by mangrove forests
  • Peaceful, scenic, and nature-rich experience
  • Ideal for photography and relaxation
  • Less crowded and highly recommended

Speed Boating

  • High-speed ride on the Godavari waters
  • Short duration adventure experience
  • Available during favorable weather conditions
  • Safety jackets provided for all riders

Note: Boating availability and routes depend on river conditions and weather. Timings and prices may vary seasonally. Visitors are advised to follow safety guidelines, wear life jackets, and book rides through authorized local operators only.
